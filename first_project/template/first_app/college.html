CHMM College for Advanced Studies, established in 1995, is located near NH 47, 3 kilometres from Paripally Junction.
The college is a private, Co-ed College, which is affiliated to UGC. Situated in lush green village town Chavarcode,
The CHMM college campus is spread over a 25-acre compound. The studiousness of the campus atmosphere is enriched by sprawling playgrounds and other recreational facilities.
